VRMedSim

VRMedSim is a virtual simulator designed to train users on how to properly connect a Fluke test device to a patient monitor. It allows users to simulate and analyze various cardiac waveforms such as normal sinus rhythm, asystole, tachycardia, and more. The simulation provides a hands-on experience in a safe, immersive environment using VR technology, perfect for biomedical engineering or clinical training scenarios.

Rehab Simulation

Designed for post-injury therapy in VR, this simulation allows patients to go through rehabilitative exercises in a virtual environment, monitored and assisted by real-time analytics.

Beneath The Piles

Beneath The Piles is a top-down 2D shooter game where players must survive relentless waves of enemies and scavenge their surroundings for resources. The goal is to outlast each wave and achieve the highest score possible, testing your reflexes, strategy, and survival instincts in a cluttered battleground filled with hidden dangers.

Electrical Safety Test

Electrical Safety Test is a 2D interactive simulation focused on proper connection and safety verification between a Fluke analyzer and medical equipment such as the NIBP monitor and applied parts. Users are guided to correctly set wires and perform safety procedures to ensure equipment compliance, ideal for biomedical engineering training or practical exam prep.

Defibrillator Simulation

Defibrillator Simulation is a 2D simulation that replicates the essential steps of operating a defibrillator. It allows users to practice the setup, electrode placement, and delivery of shocks in a controlled environment. The simulation aims to enhance user understanding of how defibrillators function during cardiac emergencies, making it a valuable tool for medical and technical training.

Belajar Jawi

Belajar Jawi is a 2D educational mobile game developed for a Game Jam event. The game introduces children to the Jawi script through interactive tracing, matching, and pronunciation activities. Designed with young learners in mind, it combines engaging visuals and sound to make learning Jawi letters fun, accessible, and culturally enriching.

Recyclash

Recyclash is a 2D educational game designed for kids to raise awareness about proper waste sorting. Players learn to differentiate between types of waste such as organic, paper, glass, and plastic by completing fast-paced sorting challenges. Through colorful visuals and interactive gameplay, Recyclash makes environmental education fun and accessible for younger audiences.
